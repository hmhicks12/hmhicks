This project will determine while 100m by 100m plots of land within Licking County are suitable for conservation. To determine suitability, these variables will be used: - Within a 100m radius of existing woodlands - Within a 100m radius of existing stream or wetland - Further than 100m from roadway - Further than 100m from railway

This first map shows the Licking County municipalities are roadways to help the view gain their barrings for the maps to come.

The two maps shown here display existing woodlands, streams and wetlands within Licking County.

This map shows suitable conservation areas within Licking County. These areas were creating using the variables listed above and the select my location feature.

Lastly, this image hypothesizes where a potential conservation area could be constructed. This area was selected simply of the bases that it was the largest visible cluster within the Licking County boundary.
